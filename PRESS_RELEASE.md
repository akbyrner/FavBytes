### Key Features of FavBytes Include:

- **Personalized Dish Logs:** Effortlessly save details about your favorite meals, including specific dishes and restaurant names.
- **Interactive Map Integration:** Powered by Mapbox, users can visually explore their dining history and find their way back to that perfect bite.
- **Modern User Experience:** Built with React 19 and DaisyUI, the platform offers a premium, responsive interface that feels alive and engaging.
- **Secure Authentication:** Recent integration of Google OAuth 2.0 ensures that your data is protected and accessible across all your devices with a single click.
